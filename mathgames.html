Math Games

Play some Exciting and fun games while understanding and mastering conecpts and skills! More games coming soon!

Grand Prix

These racing cars run on math power. How fast can you go? Practice multiplication facts and win the Grand Prix.

Puzzle Pics Multiplication

Choose one or more factors to practice. Place the puzzle piece on the missing number in the grid. Watch as the mystery picture appears.

Multiplication Blocks

Select factor blocks that multiply to the target product. Practice multiplication, factors and multiples!

Number Trails

Make a trail of factors that multiply to the given product. Double click the final factor to end the trail.

Math Monster Multiplication

Choose one or more multiplication facts to practice. Can you make all 12 monster friends appear? And stay?

Skateboard Pups

Can you add and subtract three numbers? Practice this important skill and win the skateboard race!

Jet Ski Addition

Do you know addition facts through 12? Can you add numbers in your head?

Tandem Turtles

Can you round numbers to the nearest ten?

Kitten Match

If the target is 9, which two numbers will you combine?

Sailboat Subtraction

Do you know subtraction facts through 12? Can you subtract numbers in your head?

Canoe Puppies

Can you mentally add numbers that are built with tens and ones?

Galaxy Pals 20

Help the Galaxy Pals refuel their spaceships. The total number of fuel rods is shown on the storage facility (bottom left). Each spaceship shows its own number of fuel rods. Aim the fuel blaster toward the spaceship that will make the given total.

Penguin Jump

The penguins are glacier hopping again. How fast can they go? Practice multiplication facts and power up your penguin pal.

Division Derby

The ponies are lined up and ready to start the race.Practice division facts and your pony will win first place.
